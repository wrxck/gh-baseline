# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, and disagree productively. Personal attacks, harassment, and discriminatory behaviour are not acceptable in issues, PRs, discussions, or any project-adjacent channel.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behaviour, contact the maintainer directly at **matt@heskethwebdesign.co.uk**. Reports are handled in confidence.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may, at their discretion, edit or remove comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate the Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors whose behaviour they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant 2.1 — including the canonical attribution and guideline language — is at the link above.
